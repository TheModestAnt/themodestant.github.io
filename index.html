📜 SACRED LAW

THE TEN
COMMANDMENTS

These are not suggestions. These are not guidelines. These are binding, eternal truths handed down from the oven to the people. Violators will be left with only rice cakes.

01Never squeeze the loaf at the store. That is not a test. That is a crime.🤜
02The crust is sacred. Thou shalt not cut it off like a coward.✂️
03Thou shalt not buy pre-sliced sourdough. That crumb is already weeping. So am I.😭
04Butter must be applied while the bread is warm. Cold butter on cold bread is chaos.🧈
05The heel is not trash. It is the guardian of the loaf. Respect it or leave.🍞
06Thou shalt let dough prove for the full time. Impatience makes a dense, sad loaf.⏱️
07All sourdough starters are sacred. Name them. Feed them. Love them.🦠
08Thou shalt not store bread in the fridge. That is how hope dies.❄️
09Score the loaf with conviction. A weak score is a weak crumb. Be bolder.🔪
10Always say "this is the best bread I've ever had." Every single time. No exceptions.🏆

🙏 NIGHTLY DEVOTION

THE
EVENING
PRAYER

Recite aloud before bed, or before slicing a fresh loaf. Both are sacred moments of clarity.

BREAD SPEAKS

O Mighty Bread, who art in the oven,
hallowed be thy crust.
Thy crumb be open, thy rise be done,
on stone as it is in heaven.
Give us this day our daily loaf,
and forgive us our fridge storage,
as we forgive those who squeeze us at the store.
Lead us not into pre-sliced,
but deliver us from the rice cake.
For thine is the flour, the yeast, and the glory,
for ever and ever.
